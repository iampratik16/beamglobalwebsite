# Beam Global — "PwC-style" image prompt (for Nano Banana / Gemini)

A reusable prompt template for generating realistic, editorial corporate photography
that does NOT look AI-generated. This is the recipe behind the images on the Insights
and Case Studies pages.

---

## How to use

1. Copy the **Master template** below.
2. Replace only the `{SCENE}` line with what you want in frame.
3. Replace `{ASPECT}` with the shape you need (see "Aspect cheatsheet").
4. Keep everything else — the camera/lens/lighting/realism block is what kills the
   "AI look".

---

## Master template

```
An authentic, candid editorial corporate photograph. {SCENE}.

Shot on a full-frame DSLR (Canon EOS R5) with an 85mm f/1.4 lens, natural window
daylight, soft realistic shadows, shallow depth of field with gentle background bokeh
of a real modern office. Real human skin with natural texture, pores and fine flyaway
hairs, slight natural asymmetry, relaxed authentic expressions, NO eye contact with the
camera. Muted professional colour palette, true-to-life white balance, very subtle film
grain. Looks exactly like an unretouched photograph from a real professional photo
shoot — photojournalistic, documentary realism.

Absolutely NOT AI-generated, NOT an illustration, NOT a 3D render, NO plastic or waxy
skin, NO over-sharpening, NO artificial screen glow, NO text or logos. {ASPECT}.
```

---

## Aspect cheatsheet

- Circular avatars / portraits (Insights "challenges"): `Square 1:1 composition, single subject centred`
- Text-beside-image blocks (Insights intro): `Vertical 4:5 composition`
- Wide hero / banner: `Wide 16:9 cinematic composition`
- Card thumbnail: `Landscape 3:2 composition`

---

## Example `{SCENE}` lines (proven)

- **Team collaboration (the loved insights image):**
  "a small diverse team of three business professionals collaborating around a glass
  meeting table in a bright modern office, one person pointing at a laptop showing
  charts, printed reports, notebooks and coffee cups on the table"

- **Client challenges:**
  "a businesswoman in her early 40s in a charcoal blazer standing near a window in a
  modern office, holding a phone to her ear with a thoughtful, concerned expression"

- **Vendor challenges:**
  "a man in his late 30s in a light blue button-up shirt sitting at a desk, focused on
  his laptop with one hand near his chin, a coffee mug and paper notebook beside him"

---

## Tips that matter most for realism

- Always name a **real camera + lens + aperture** (e.g. "Canon EOS R5, 85mm f/1.4").
- Always include **"no eye contact with camera"** and **"candid"** — posed-to-camera
  reads as stock/AI.
- Always include the **negative block** ("NOT AI-generated… NO plastic skin… NO screen
  glow"). Glowing dashboards are the #1 tell.
- Ask for **subtle film grain** and **natural skin texture / pores** — perfect skin is
  the giveaway.
- Keep a **muted, true-to-life palette**. Over-saturated, over-lit = AI.
